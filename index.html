How travel agency works ?

A travel agency is a business that helps customers plan and book their trips by acting as an intermediary between travelers and service providers like airlines, hotels, and tour operators. The process typically begins with a consultation where the customer shares their travel preferences, followed by the agency researching and recommending options based on the customerâ€™s budget and interests. After the customer selects their preferred options, the agency handles all bookings, including flights, accommodations, and activities. Payment is processed, and the agency provides ongoing support during the trip if any issues arise. Finally, post-trip follow-ups may be conducted to gather feedback. Whether offering vacation packages, customized itineraries, or group tours, travel agencies simplify the travel planning process, offering convenience and expert advice.
